HVAC Ontology

According to the description of the HVAC in the different pilots, the Platoon HVAC ontology aims to represent the HVAC Systems, its components and the existing relations between them. 

This ontology may overlap with the following ontologies and should minimize and align with :

  • Brick (https://brickschema.org/)
  • Saref for building (https://saref.etsi.org/saref4bldg/v1.1.2/)

Then, the HVAC ontology defines systems relative to cooling, ventilation and air conditioning systems in building, their subsystems such as heating valve, cooling valve and the relationships between them. The HVAC is a system has three subsystems: the plt:HeatingSystem that is related by the relation plt:hasHeatingSystem, the plt:CoolingSystem that is related by the relation plt:hasCoolingSystem and the plt:VentilationSystem that is related by the relation plt:hasVentilationSystem. Saref:HVAC is located (s4bldg:is-ContainedIn) in s4bldg:Building. This HVAC is controlled by a plt:BuildingManagementSystem that controls (brick:controls) the HVAC.

The cooling system and heating system has respectively plt:CoolingValve and plt:HeatingValve that are their subsystem (seas:subSystemOf). Sometime, in HVAC system there is also an plt:AirHandlerUnit that is a seas:subSystemOf the saref:HVAC. This air handler is connected to the heating, the cooling, and the ventilation systems. A boiler (plt:Boiler) or heating coil (plt:HeatingCoil) can be subSystem of the heating system and the chiller (plt:Chiller) or cooling coil (plt:CoolingCoil) is a subSystem of cooling system. The plt:Fancoil that has a plt:Fan and s4bldg:Coil as a subsystem, is connected to the air handler unit.
